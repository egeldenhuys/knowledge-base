Restart Cinnamon
================

1. Press `Ctrl + Alt + F1` to open the text terminal
2. `cinnamon --replace --display=:0`
3. Switch to the GUI using `Ctrl + Alt + F8`


Alternatively you can press `Alt + F2` and enter `r` to restart Cinnamon.
